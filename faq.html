Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Please wear semi-formal attire. The event will be held outdoors, so please dress accordingly.

Can I bring a plus one?

We would love to accommodate everyone, but due to limited space, we are only able to allow those guests formally invited on your invitation.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will start at 11:00 AM, so we recommend arriving by 10:30 AM to find your seat and settle in.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is ample parking available at the venue. Please follow the signs upon arrival.

Will there be an open bar?

Yes, we will have an open bar available during the cocktail hour and reception.
